Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the U.S. around 1905, brought by Mexican migrants working in the Southwest, with early mentions in newspapers describing them as spicy street food sold by vendors in Los Angeles and San Antonio's "Chili Queens"
